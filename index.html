About Me

Welcome to my academic profile! I am Dr. Mudasir Ahmad Wani, a dedicated researcher in Natural Language Processing (NLP) currently working at Prince Sultan University, KSA. With a strong academic background and a passion for innovative research, I strive to make meaningful contributions to the field of computer science.

Research

My research focuses on the extraction and analysis of social data, employing various statistical, machine/deep learning, and NLP techniques to develop predictive models. I am committed to advancing the field and have contributed to numerous projects and publications.
