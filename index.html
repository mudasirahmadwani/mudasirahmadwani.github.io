About Me

I extend a warm welcome to my academic profile. I am Dr. Mudasir Ahmad Wani, a committed researcher specializing in the dynamic field of Natural Language Processing (NLP). Presently, I am engaged in pioneering research endeavors at Prince Sultan University in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. My academic journey has been fortified by a robust foundation in computer science, and my unwavering commitment to innovative research propels me to pursue endeavors that advance the frontiers of knowledge. Before my current role, I had the privilege of serving as a lecturer and researcher at the esteemed Norwegian University of Science and Technology in Norway. These experiences have shaped my academic and professional trajectory, providing invaluable insights into the multifaceted realm of computer science. I look forward to collaborating and contributing to the ever-evolving landscape of NLP and Artificial Intelligence, with the aspiration of making substantive contributions that resonate with the global research community.

Research

I enjoy mining and analyzing online data and design systems to understand, detect, and predict, anti-social behavior, thus building a better and safer social media space.
My areas of research expertise encompass Text Mining, Computational Linguistics, Natural Language Processing, Deep Learning, Emotion Mining, and Data Science. In addition to my teaching responsibilities in computer science, I have provided guidance and mentorship to over 30 master's thesis students at prestigious institutions such as NTNU in Norway, Jamia Millia Islamia in New Delhi, and Prince Sultan Universit, KSA serving as a research supervisor and contributing to the academic development of emerging scholars
