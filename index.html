About Me

Welcome to my academic profile! I am Dr. Mudasir Ahmad Wani, a dedicated researcher in Natural Language Processing (NLP) currently working at Prince Sultan University, KSA. With a strong academic background and a passion for innovative research, I strive to make meaningful contributions to the field of computer science.

Research

I enjoy mining and analyzing online data and design systems to understand, detect, and predict, anti-social behavior, thus building a better and safer social media space.

My areas of research expertise encompass Text Mining, Natural Language Processing, Deep Learning, Emotion Mining, and Data Science. In addition to my teaching responsibilities in computer science, I have provided guidance and mentorship to over 30 master's thesis students at prestigious institutions such as NTNU in Norway, Jamia Millia Islamia in New Delhi, and Prince Sultan Universit, KSA serving as a research supervisor and contributing to the academic development of emerging scholars
